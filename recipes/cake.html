Mediterranean Stuffed Zucchini

Description:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Zucchini
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the Zucchini.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. stuff the zucchini according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the zucchini rest before serving.
